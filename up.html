Playgrounds in University Park.

Adventure Playground-The most popular playground and fits for all age!!

Location: 1 Beach Tree Lane, Next to University community Park.

Age: All ages

Time: 10am to 6 pm

There is a ping-pong table, which you can play ping-pong. There is also a big playground contains super fun stuff like a slides and climbing webs. Next to the castle-like playground, there are some super big legos and a huge chess board.

University Community Park Playground

1 Beach Tree Lane

Age: 4-12 years old

Time: All Day

There are three big parts of the playground and some little stuff like spinner. There is a broad lawn beside it, which looks very relaxing.

Dave Robbins Park

Location: 5075 Tamarack Way

Age: 4-12 years old

Time: All Day

There are a couple of fun climbing and obstacle-like elements in the playground which would be super fun.

William.R.Mason Regional Park

Location: 18712 University Dr

Age: 3 and 3+

Time: 7am to 9pm

There are multiple playgrounds fit for all age of kids. In the park, there are also many lanes and lakes and awesome views for parents.
